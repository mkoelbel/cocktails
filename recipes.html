Old-Fashioned

Ingredients:

  • 1 tsp sugar
  • dash angostura bitters
  • dash water
  • 2 oz Whiskey
  • flamed orange peel
  • maraschino cherry

Instructions:

  1. Pour sugar, bitters, and water into glass and mix together.
  2. Put ice in glass.
  3. Pour whiskey over ice.
  4. Flame orange peel, coat rim with orange peel, and place peel in glass.
  5. Garnish with cherry.

Manhattan

Ingredients:

  • 3/4 oz sweet vermouth
  • 2 oz whiskey
  • dash angostura bitters
  • maraschino cherry

Instructions:

  1. Pour whiskey and sweet vermouth into glass.
  2. Add dash of bitters.
  3. Put ice in glass.
  4. Garnish with cherry.

Mojito

(Double Batch)

Ingredients:

  • 3 limes (3 oz lime juice - reserve wedges for garnish)
  • 2 Tbsp superfine sugar
  • 2 sprigs mint
  • 4 oz white rum (optional: 3 oz)
  • club soda (optional: seltzer)

Instructions:

  1. In a shaker, combine lime juice, sugar, and mint until sugar is dissolved.
  2. Add rum and stir.
  3. Fill tall glasses with ice.
  4. Strain cocktail into glasses.
  5. Top off glasses with club soda.
  6. Garnish with mint and lime wedge.
  7. Give final stir.

Margarita

(Double Batch)

Ingredients:

  • salt
  • 1 lime (reserve wedges for garnish)
  • 3/4 oz orange liqueur
  • 4 oz tequila

Instructions:

  1. Rub rim of glass with lime wedge, dip rim in salt, and chill glass in fridge.
  2. In shaker, combine lime juice, orange liqueur, and tequlia, and shake.
  3. Put ice in glass.
  4. Pour cocktail over ice.
  5. Garnish with lime wedge.

Whiskey Sour

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 lemon
  • 3/4 oz simple syrup
  • 1 egg
  • 2 oz whiskey
  • dash angostura bitters

Instructions:

  1. In shaker, combine lemon juice, simple syrup, egg white, and whiskey, and dry shake.
  2. Add ice, and wet shake.
  3. Place ice in glass.
  4. Strain cocktail into glass.
  5. Add a dash of bitters.

Bee's Knees

(Double Batch)

Ingredients:

  • 1 lemon
  • 1 oz honey syrup
  • 4 oz gin

Instructions:

  1. Chill glass in fridge.
  2. In shaker, combine lemon juice, honey syrup, gin, and ice, and shake.
  3. Strain cocktail into glass.

Amarreto Sour

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 lemon
  • 1 tsp simple syrup (optional: maple syrup)
  • 1 egg
  • 1 1/2 oz amaretto
  • 1/2 oz whiskey
  • dash angostura bitters
  • maraschino cherry

Instructions:

  1. In shaker, combine lemon juice, syrup, egg white, amaretto, whiskey, and bitters, and dry shake.
  2. Add ice, and wet shake.
  3. Place ice in glass.
  4. Strain cocktail into glass.
  5. Garnish with cherry.

Whiskey and Amaretto

  1. Put ice in glass.

Pina Colada

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz white rum
  • 3 oz cream of coconut
  • 4 oz pineapple juice
  • maraschino cherry (optional)

Instructions:

(Frozen)

  1. In blender, combine rum, cream of coconut, pineapple juice, and 2 cups of ice, and blend.
  2. Pour cocktail into tall glass.
  3. Garnish with cherry.

(Shaken)

  1. In shaker, combine rum, cream of coconut, pineapple juice, and ice, and shake.
  2. Strain cocktail into glass.
  3. Garnish with cherry.

Gin and Tonic

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz gin
  • 4 oz club soda
  • lime wedge

Instructions:

  1. Put ice in glass.
  2. Pour gin and club soda over ice.
  3. Garnish with lime wedge.

Baileys White Russian

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz vodka
  • 1 oz Kahlua coffee liqueur
  • 1 oz Baileys Irish cream

Instructions:

  1. Add vodka and Kahlua to glass.
  2. Add ice.
  3. Top with Baileys.
  4. Gently stir (don't fully combine).
