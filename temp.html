Aviation

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 lemon
  • 1/2 oz Maraschino liqueur
  • 1/2 oz Creme de violette liqueur
  • 1 1/2 oz gin
  • maraschino cherry (optional: flamed lemon peel)

Instructions:

  1. Chill glass.
  2. Add ice to shaker.
  3. Add lemon juice, both liqueurs, and gin to shaker.
  4. Shake.
  5. Strain cocktail into glass.
  6. Garnish with cherry or lemon peel.
