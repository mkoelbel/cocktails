Jameson Old Fashioned

Ingredients:

  • 1 tsp sugar
  • dash angostura bitters
  • splash club soda
  • 2 oz Jameson Irish Whiskey
  • flamed orange peel

Instructions:

  1. Pour sugar, bitters, and club soda into glass, and mix together.
  2. Add ice to glass.
  3. Pour whiskey over ice.
  4. Flame orange peel, coat rim with juice from orange peel, and place peel in glass.
