Hugo Spritz

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 oz St-Germain Elderflower Liqueur
  • 4 oz Prosecco
  • 1 oz club soda
  • 1 sprig mint
  • lemon wheel

Instructions:

  1. Add St-Germain and mint sprig to glass.
  2. Muddle and then let sit for 3 minutes.
  3. Add ice, Prosecco, and club soda to glass.
  4. Stir briefly and gently.
  5. Garnish with mint sprig and lemon wheel.
